About GHS PINAVOORKUDY

GHS PINAVOORKUDY was established in 1978 and is managed by the Department of Education. Located in the rural Kothamangalam block of Ernakulam district, Kerala, the school offers classes from Grade 1 to 10. It is a co-educational institution with Malayalam as the medium of instruction.

The school has a well-maintained government building with 7 classrooms, a dedicated headmaster's office, and additional rooms for non-teaching activities. The campus is equipped with electricity, a functional well for drinking water, and separate toilets for boys and girls.

Facilities include a library with 700 books, a computer lab with 4 functional computers, and a mid-day meal program. Despite the lack of a playground, the school ensures a quality learning environment for all students.
